Guard Young Hearts · Care for Left-Behind Children Charity Program

Fill the gap with company, warm childhood with love

Current Situation

In remote rural areas, many children stay in hometown while their parents work in cities far away. These kids lack daily family care. Some struggle with mental health problems, insufficient school supplies and after-school guidance.

Our Charity Projects

1. Material Donation: Collect books, stationery and warm clothes for rural primary schools;
2. Online Tutoring: Volunteers provide free remote homework guidance;
3. Field Visit: Volunteers visit villages and organize fun outdoor activities on holidays.

How You Can Help

Donate unused children’s books or new school supplies. You can also sign up as an online volunteer in your spare time. Every small kindness brings hope to disadvantaged kids in countryside.

Become Our Volunteer

Everyone with kindness can join us no matter your age or job. Use your free time to help disadvantaged children grow happily. Our volunteer registration is open all year round.
